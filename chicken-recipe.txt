Chicken Tenderloin Recipe:
Ingredients:
2 lbs of chicken tenderloin
salt
favorite seasoning
water
pan
bowl that will hold the chicken

Directions:
1. In bowl, mix 1 handful of salt with 4 cups of water.
2. Add chicken to bowl and let set for 15 min up to 6 hrs.
	...called brining(makes meat juicy)
3.remove chicken, rinse off with water, add seasonings.
4.in an oiled pan cook chicken 1 min per side on medium high heat
5.cook chicken on medium low heat and additional 6 minutes turning every 3 min
6.let set for 5 min and serve
